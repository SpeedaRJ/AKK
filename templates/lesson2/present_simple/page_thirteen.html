Questions
The word order in questions is the following:
DO + PERSON + VERB + THE REST?
Besedni vrstni red v vprašanjih je naslednji: DO + OSEBA + GLAGOL + OSTALO?
Here’s an example:
DO + PERSON + VERB + THE REST?
Do you play football?
There are two forms of questions: yes/no questions and wh-questions.
Poznamo dve obliki vprašanj: ja/ne vprašanja in wh-vprašanja.
