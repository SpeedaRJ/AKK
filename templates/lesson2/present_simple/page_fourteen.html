Yes/no questions are questions you can answer with yes or no. To answer yes/no questions, you can use the short form:
Yes, I do. No, I do not./No, I don’t,
or the long form:
Yes, I do play football. No, I do not play football./No, I don’t play football.
Ja/ne vprašanja so vprašanja, na katera lahko odgovoriš z ja ali ne.
Za odgovore na omenjena vprašanja lahko uporabimo kratko ali dolgo obliko.
In questions, the third person singular does not get the -s suffix.
Pri vprašanjih glagol za tretjo osebo ednine nima končnice -s.
Do you play football?
Yes, I do.
No, I do not.
Yes, I do play football.
No, I do not play football.
