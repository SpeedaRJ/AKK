If a verb ends in -y and if there is a consonant preceding the -y, the -y turns into -ies in the third person singular.
Če se glagol konča z -y in če pred -y stoji soglasnik, se -y v tretji osebi ednine spremeni v -ies.
I cry if you punch me.
Jaz jočem, če me udariš.
He cries if you punch him.
Joče, če ga udariš.
Verbs ending in -y, when the -y is preceded by a vowel, the usual -s suffix is added in third person singular.
Pri glagolih, ki se končajo na -y in pri katerih pred -y stoji samoglasnik, se pri tretji osebi ednine doda končnica -s.
I play the violin. She plays the piano.
