When adding -s, we need to be careful if the verb ends in -s, -ss, -x, -o, -ch or -sh.
In that case, we add the suffix -es and pronounce the end of the word as ‘iz’ not ‘s’.
Here are two examples: She kisses him every day. He always brushes his teeth.
Ko dodajamo končnico -s, moramo paziti, če se glagol konča na -s, -ss, -x, -o, -ch ali -sh.
V tem primeru dodamo končnico -es in izgovorimo konec besede kot ‘iz’, ne kot ‘s’.
Tukaj je primer: Poljubi ga vsak dan. Vedno si umije zobe.
