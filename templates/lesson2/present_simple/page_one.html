Now, we are going to learn some grammar.
Zdaj se bova naučila nekaj slovnice.
When we talk about the present, our routines, habits, everyday activities, states and general truths we use a tense called Present Simple.
Ko govorimo o sedanjosti, dnevnih rutinah, navadah, vsakodnevnih aktivnostih, stanjih in splošnih resnicah, uporabimo čas, ki se imenuje Present Simple (sedanjik).
Here is an example for each category:
habits: I never drink milk.
everyday activities: I go to school every day.
states: I like flowers.
general truths: I breathe through my nose.
navade: Nikoli ne pijem mleka.
vsakodnevne aktivnosti: Vsak dan grem v šolo.
stanje: Všeč so mi rože.
splošne resnice: Diham skozi nos.
