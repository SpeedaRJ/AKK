When we make negative sentences, we use the structure “do not” or shorter “don’t” before the verb.
Ko tvorimo nikalne povedi, pred glagolom uporabljamo strukturo “do not” ali krajše “don’t”.
I don’t drive a car.
Ne vozim avta
Before the third person singular we use the structure “does not” or shorter “doesn’t”.
Pred tretjo osebo ednine uporabimo strukturo “does not” ali krajše “doesn’t”.
