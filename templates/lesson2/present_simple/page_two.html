We form sentences like this:
person + verb + the rest.
Povedi tvorimo tako:
oseba + glagol + ostalo.
Here is an example:
I drive a car
Tukaj je primer: Vozim avto.
The words and phrases that help us recognize the Present Simple are: always, never, sometimes, often, rarely, usually, every day, every week, ...
Besede, ki nam pomagajo prepoznati in tvoriti Present Simple so: vedno, nikoli, včasih, pogosto, redko, ponavadi, vsak dan, vsak teden, …
Here is an example:
I drive to work every day.
Tukaj je primer: Na delo se vozim vsak dan.
