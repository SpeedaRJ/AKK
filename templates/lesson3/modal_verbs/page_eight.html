2. MUST
. (-)
She mustn’t go now.= Ona ne sme iti.
She doesn’t have to go now.= Njej ni treba iti.
?
Does she have to go now?
As you can see, when we negate the sentence, ‘must not’ and ‘does not have to’ are not interchangeable because they carry different meanings. In questions we can use either of them and the meaning stays the same.
Kot vidiš, ‘must not’ in ‘does not have to’ nista zamenljiva v nikalnih povedih, saj imata različne pomene. V vprašanjih lahko uporabimo enega ali drugega in pri tem pomen ostaja enak.
