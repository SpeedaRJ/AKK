Modal verbs are a special group of verbs that we use to express the following: ability, obligation, permission, possibility, requests and advice. Modal verbs: can, may, might, must, have to, should.
Modalni glagoli so posebna skupina glagolov, ki se uporabljajo, da izrazimo naslednje: sposobnost, dolžnost, dovoljenje, možnost, zahteve in nasvete. Modalni glagoli: can, may, might, must, have to, should
Ability - zmožnost, sposobnost
I can swim.
Znam plavati.
I can’t dance.
Ne znam plesati.
I can’t hear you.
Ne slišim te.
