Mogoče je v službi?
When we use ‘may’, there is a stronger possibility that what we are saying is true. When we use ‘might’ the possibility that what we are saying is true, is not so strong.
Ko uporabimo ‘may’, je možnost, da je to, kar smo povedali, resnično, večja kot če uporabimo ’might’.
